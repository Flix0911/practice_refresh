About Quinoa Salad

Looking for something health, light, but full of tasty treats? Quinoa Salad will never disappoint! It of course has the star, quinoa, but is complimented by avocado, beans, cucumbers, some tomatoes, cheese, and topped with cilantro. This 'ancient grain', will provide you everything you'll ever need

Ingrediants:

  • Quinoa
  • Cucumbers
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Avocado
  • Cilantro
